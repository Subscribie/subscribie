Privacy Policy

Subscribie

This privacy policy explains how Subscribie collects, uses and shares your personal data when you visit or use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws.

What data do we collect?

We collect personal data that you provide to us when you register, Subscribie, purchase or contact us through the website. This may include your name, email address, phone number, billing, payment details and any other information you choose to share with us.

We also collect some data automatically when you use the website, such as your IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, pages visited, time spent, links clicked and other usage data like cookies.

How do we use your data?

  • To provide you with the services you request, such as automating and processing your subscriptions and one-time orders, managing your shop and customers and responding to your inquiries.
  • To communicate with you about your shop, services, time-sensitive issues like failed payments, and other events that may be of interest to you.
  • To improve your experience on the website, such as displaying relevant content in your dashboard and shop.
  • To maintain and secure the website, such as preventing fraud, spam, abuse and unauthorized access.
  • To comply with our legal obligations, such as tax, accounting and reporting requirements.
  • To use this information for purposes that benefit both you and us, such as improving our products and services, providing you with relevant updates, and conducting research and analysis.

How do we share your data?

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We use Stripe as our third-party payment processor to help us provide our services to you and process your personal data. Stripe may also use your personal data for its own purposes, such as fraud prevention, security and compliance. Please review Stripe's privacy policy to learn more.

When you make a payment through our website or app, we share your personal data with Stripe, such as your name, email address, payment card details, billing address and transaction information. Stripe may also collect additional personal data from you, such as your device information, IP address and location data.

Stripe may share your personal data with other third parties who help Stripe provide its services to us or who have a legal right or obligation to access it. These may include:

  • Stripe's service providers, such as analytics providers and marketing partners.
  • Stripe's business partners, such as banks, card networks and payment schemes who enable Stripe to process payments and payouts.
  • Stripe's professional advisors, such as lawyers, accountants and auditors who may need to access Stripe's data for legal or regulatory purposes.
  • Stripe's regulators, law enforcement agencies and courts who may request or require Stripe's data for compliance or investigation purposes.

Stripe requires all third parties who handle your personal data on its behalf to respect its confidentiality and security and to comply with the GDPR and other applicable laws.

What are your rights?

You have the following rights in relation to your personal data:

  • The right to access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you at any time.
  • The right to rectification: You can request us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.
  • The right to erasure: You can request us to delete or remove any personal data we hold about you that is no longer necessary for our purposes or that you withdraw your consent for.
  • The right to restriction: You can request us to limit or suspend the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances, such as if you dispute its accuracy or legality.
  • The right to portability: You can request us to transfer your personal data to another service provider in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format.
  • The right to object: You can object to our processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes or for any other reason based on your specific situation.
  • The right to withdraw consent: You can withdraw your consent for any processing of your personal data that is based on your consent at any time.
To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details provided below. We will respond to your request within one month of receiving it. We may ask you to verify your identity before taking any action on your request.
